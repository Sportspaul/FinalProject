Do you fall asleep at inconvenient times?

The Problem

Sleep, alongside food and water are physiological needs, and biological requirements to function properly as a human. If these requirements are not sufficiently met, you can’t perform optimal in other aspects of life. Managing your everyday tasks can be challenging, sleep becomes underpriortized, and getting enough might be difficult. Have you ever fallen asleep in class, on the bus or in front of the tv while watching your favourite show? Maybe you needed a quick powernap to get your energy level back up but slept to long, or maybe you overslept and missed an important meeting. Regardless sleep is necessary, and you deserve to be able to control your day and have a good night sleep. We believe that a technological device can solve these problems and ultimately improve your health.

Our product

Our solution is SleepLezz, a wearable wristband. The idea is that the users should get better control over their sleeping habits. The wristband wakes you up by vibrating, and if the user is not responding to the vibrations SleepLezz will give the user an electric shock. For some people the fear of the shock is enough to stay awake, but for others the adrenaline produced after a shock will provide them with the boost they need. The shock is not designed to hurt you, but it must be strong enough to wake you. The purpose is to make you aware of your actions, and program your brain to stay awake through negative conditioning. Our product will use the same principles to keep you from falling asleep in class, on the bus or in front of the tv while watching your favourite show. SleepLezz uses heart rate data to get detailed info from your sleep. While an accelerometer measures how much you move during the night. Bluetooth connects the app and the device. The device has a chargeable battery with battery life up to 5 days with normal use. The SleepLezz wristband is compatible with an app. By using the companion app, you will be able to control when you want to sleep and when not to.

Our vision is to wake you up feeling rested and awake. Give you full control over your sleeping pattern. Go to bed feeling tired and wake up fully rested ready for a new beautiful day.

Application

Home Screen

In the app you control your settings and set up the wristband to your liking. The app features include: alarm, sleepstats and Powernap buddy. In addition to the main features you have three buttons on top of the screen.

Mute - Don’t disturb mode, for a full night of sleep without any distractions.
Settings - Set up the app and wristband to your liking.
Blue Light Filter - Give your eyes a break.

Sleep

Alarm that wakes you up to a tune of your choosing. If not, the wristband will wake you up with a vibration or a small but effective electrical shock. The alarm feature lets you set a time to wake up, but your wearable device uses your sleep data to wake you up at an appropriate time according your sleep cycle, in order to avoid grogginess.

Sleep Analysis

The wristband will monitor when and how long you remain in the different sleeping stages and sends data to the application on your phone. The application will give you a graphical presentation of your night of sleep. The application will store your sleeping data, enabling the opportunity to see pattern over a period of time.

Powernap Buddy

A powernap has proven benefits like improved cognitive skills, memory, creativity and it gives you a great energy boost, but only if you do it the right way. Power napping can be hard, if you fall into deep sleep you will most likely wake up feeling groggier than before. This function will help you wake up feeling more energized. Sleeping for too long will also have a negative effect on your ability to fall asleep at night. Therefore the application. will only allow you to set a timer for a maximum of 20 minutes. SleepLezz will also make sure you get the most out of your powernap by starting your timer the exact moment you fall asleep.

Theories

The functionality of SleepLezz is inspired by the American psychologist B. F. Skinner’s theory on negative reinforcement. Negative Punishment after a specific behaviour will train your brain to not repeat it. Negative punishment is most effective when it immediately follows a response and it’s applied consistently. Sleeplezz causes a negative punishment, in the form of an electric shock after an unwanted behaviour, that is falling asleep. By design our products punishment is both consistent and occurs immediately after unwanted behaviour.

Our product is also inspired by the American psychologist Abramham Maslow’s theories of human motivation and development. Maslow describes a pyramid of needs, that is important for every human. The hierarchy is divided into 5 stages, and the bottom stages must be fulfilled before moving on to the next. The last stages consist of self-actualization and esteem. On the bottom of the pyramid are the physiological need. Among food, water and shelter we find sleep.
